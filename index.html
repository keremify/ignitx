Getting started

Three steps, zero installs.

01
🌐
Open Instagram
Go to instagram.com and make sure you're logged in. Disable your ad blocker on this domain.
02
🛠
Open DevTools
Press F12 or Ctrl+Shift+J (Win) / ⌘+⌥+I (Mac) and click the Console tab.
03
⚡
Paste & Run
Copy any script below, paste it into the console and press Enter. A UI panel slides in on the right.
⚠ If pasting is blocked, type allow pasting and press Enter first, then paste your script.
Always disable your ad blocker on instagram.com — it breaks session tokens.
